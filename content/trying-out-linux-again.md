## The linux journey

As is tradition when it comes time to switch to linux. I naturally decided to start with Ubuntu. More specifically 20.04, the latest realase at the time.

The inital experience was plesent, even more so than I expected. For one the boot sequence now displayed the Ubuntu logo beneath the usually motherboard vender logo, similar to how windows will display its dotted spinner during the boot process. This may seem pretty minor but its these little touches that really make me smile when trying out new software.. It's nice knowing someone out there on the dev team thought about this tiny UX feature.
